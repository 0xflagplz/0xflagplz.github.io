Port Scanning with Beacons

Beacon has a port scanner for target discovers
portscan <hosts> [ports] [discovery] [max]
Arguments
    • - hostsis a range of targets
      • 192.168.0.1/24 , 172.16.4.25-172.16.4.100
    • - portsis a range of ports to scan
      • 1-1024, 5900, 8000-9000
    • - discoveris a method to check if a host is alive
      • arp, icmp, or none
    • - maxis the maximum number of sockets open at once
      • 4 for Windows XP is OK, 1024for Windows 7 and later
